I love learning

I am a soon to be graduating senior who enjoys learning. With hobbies in computers, puzzles, and much more. I love to try new things as I move forward in life. “The Only Easy Day Was Yesterday” - US Navy SEALs. This quote about never giving up is a strong, personal favorite of mine. To me, this quote embodies the idea that no matter how much something in front of you sucks, you should always keep going because it will eventually get better. I, Nicolas (Nic), am a simple person who enjoys many hobbies. Hobbies that some might find strange, but they bring me joy nonetheless. Of these hobbies, some of my favorite include computers/cybersecurity, Dungeons & Dragons, and Track & Field. These hobbies have much to do with who I am as a person and have helped shape my personality, someone who enjoys a challenge and likes to see how things work. These personality traits have led me to be the kind of person who will often sit and watch, observing the things around me and taking mental notes, always looking for a solution to whatever problem is processing in the back of my head.
