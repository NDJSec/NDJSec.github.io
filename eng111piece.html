English 111 Piece: Overview

  • What did the assignment ask you to do?
    • In this piece from English 111 I was tasked with creating a small essay where I had to review and comment on a music album of my choice. For this assignment I choose Pitbull's Planet Pit where I reviewed and analyzed Pitbull's choice in words as well as some of the underlying meanings of his lyrics. In the end, this piece was used to define the Planet Pit ablum and the role that I thought it played in the overall music scene during early 2010.
  • What did you do well on the assignment?
    • I felt that I had a strong analysis of the Planet Pit album, as well as overviewing Pitbull's past and how that plays into his music. Furthermore, I felt that I did a good job of packing the important information into the given space, while still keeping it understandable.
  • What would you have done differently?
    • If I could go back and do parts differently I would most likely clean up some of the analysis and add more. While, I thought what I had was good analysis I feel that there is probably more detail that I could have added if I had the space. Also, I would go back at tryign to hook the reader a bit better in the beginning, but definitly in the end. I feel that the end could have been stronger in providing a better overall piece for the reader.
  • What did you learn?
    • I learned how to write a review of a piece of music/literature, as well as what goes into a good or bad review. Also, the different levels of bais and what to avoid when writing a review so that it stays as unbiased as possible.
