Goal Reflections

Furthermore, I completed my goal of getting into a college that I am happy with and am excited to go to in the fall. On the academic side of things, I completed my goal of finshing strong and completing all my class with an outcome that I am happy with. Overall, I am happy with how high school was as a whole. In the end, while I am ready to leave high school I can say that there are definently people and organizations that I will miss once I leave.
